The Wendlerizer

The Standard program is for most people. It moves the lower body lifts up by 10 and the upper body up by 5 every 3 weeks.

The "Smaller jumps" program will make more modest jumps after every 531 week. Recommended for those who are finding it difficult to keep up with the upper body lifts.

One Rep Max Calculator

A reasonably useful way to calculate a one rep max is to use a multi-rep max lift plugged into the formula:

1RM = (weight_lifted * num_of_reps * 0.033) + weight_lifted.
