The Wendlerizer Mad gains since 2013

The program generator uses your best lifts to program out eight weeks of lifting based on Jim Wendler's 5/3/1 and Beyond 5/3/1 books. It includes accessory lifts and instructions for use.

The Standard program is for most people. Every three weeks it moves the lower body lifts up by 10 pounds and the upper body up by 5 pounds.

To decrease the pace of weight increases after each microcycle, the "Smaller jumps" program makes more modest jumps, and is recommended for those who are finding it difficult to keep up with the upper body lifts. Click the "Make small jumps" button to generate this program instead of the standard.

If you have run this program to completion and are looking to recalculate, add 20 pounds to the Squat and Deadlift 1RMs that you entered last time (listed at the top of your program), and 10 pounds to the Press and Bench Press. (10/5 if running the small jumps program). Take a week off or with 50% weights and decreased volume in-between cycles.
